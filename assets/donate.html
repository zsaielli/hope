Why Your Support Matters

Hope Family Care Center has been a cornerstone of health in Kansas City for decades. We serve patients who are uninsured, underinsured, and underserved — offering primary care, behavioral health, women's health, and more.

Your donation helps ensure that cost is never a barrier to getting the care a family needs.

  • ✓ $25 covers a well-child visit for a child with no insurance
  • ✓ $50 provides diabetes management support for an uninsured adult
  • ✓ $100 funds a behavioral health session for a family in crisis
  • ✓ Monthly gifts create lasting, sustained impact in our community
