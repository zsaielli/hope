Answer
the Call

Volunteer with the underprivileged as a Healthcare Professional or Administrative Assistant and bring hope to Kansas City's east side.

“

And if anyone gives even a cup of cold water to one of these little ones who is my disciple, truly I tell you, that person will certainly not lose their reward.

Hope Family Care Center relies on the invaluable contributions of dedicated volunteers who passionately support our mission of providing top-tier, personalized healthcare. We are looking for volunteers with a compassionate heart to volunteer at our medical clinic.

We Have Opportunities For

The following services

🩺
Clinicians
Medical Volunteers

Hope Family Care Center is currently seeking licensed physicians, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants interested in a volunteer opportunity of half a day per week or every other week. In addition, we are also looking for qualified nurses, medical assistants, and healthy lifestyle educators to volunteer their expertise for the same time commitment.

If you possess a strong passion for providing quality healthcare and improving the overall well-being of those living in Kansas City, Missouri's east side, we welcome you to become a part of our growing team.

🗓️ Half day per week or every other week
📋
Administrative Assistants
Patient Reception & Support

If you have administrative assistant skills, we welcome your willingness to volunteer your time. As a patient reception assistant, you will play a valuable role in helping us maintain efficient operations by performing patient check-ins and check-outs, managing phone calls, scheduling appointments, and maintaining patient records.

When you join the Hope Family Care Center volunteer team, you will make a meaningful impact on those in need while gaining hands-on experience in the healthcare industry.

🗓️ Once a week or every other week — half day
