About Hope Family Care

Quality care for those
who need it most

Hope Family Care Center was founded on a simple mission: to honor God by providing quality personal health care to the people of Kansas City who need it most.

We know many of you have a hard time finding a place to live, finding transportation, getting food, and getting the medical care you need. We are here to walk alongside you and connect you to real, practical help.

Our mission is your whole health—body, mind, and spirit. We'll help you navigate the healthcare system, understand your options, and build toward a healthier future.

Recognition & Accreditation

Trusted Community Healthcare

What our credentials mean for you, in plain language.

HRSA Supported Health Center

We receive funding and support from the Health Resources & Services Administration, a federal agency dedicated to improving healthcare access in underserved communities.

Federally Qualified Health Center

As an FQHC, we meet rigorous federal standards for quality and safety, and offer sliding-scale fees based on what you can afford.

Patient-Centered Care

Every treatment plan starts with you. We listen first, then build care around your needs, your culture, and your life.

Experienced Medical Team

Our providers bring years of clinical experience in family medicine, pediatrics, and whole-person community health.

Affordable Healthcare

No insurance? No problem. We offer sliding-scale fees and accept most major insurance plans, including Medicaid and Medicare.

Quality & Safety

We've earned national recognition for our commitment to clinical quality, patient safety, and using technology to improve care.
