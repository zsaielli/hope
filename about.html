Who We Are

A Community of Care in
Kansas City's East Side

We know many of you in Kansas City face real challenges — finding stable housing, reliable transportation, enough food, and access to quality medical care. At Hope Family Care Center, we're here to help on all fronts.

Our team personally connects with every patient. We don't just treat your health — we get to know you, speak your language, and walk alongside you. We help you keep appointments, manage medications, and make sustainable lifestyle changes that lead to a happier, healthier life.

We also connect you with social workers and community resources so that your whole life, not just your health, can move forward.

Our Programs

Comprehensive Care for the Whole Person

COVID-19 Care & Prevention

Get tested quickly and stop the spread. If COVID-19 has affected you, we provide care and treatment for symptoms, complications, and recovery — so everyone has access to the help they need.

Chronic Disease Management

Living with hypertension, diabetes, obesity, asthma, or HIV? We connect you with programs that provide medication support, diet and exercise guidance, blood sugar management, and counseling.

Community Wraparound Services

From food and housing to transportation and language assistance, our social workers help you navigate the resources you need to stabilize and thrive — not just survive.

Appointment & Medication Support

We help you keep your appointments and make sure you understand and take your medications correctly — because consistent care is the foundation of lasting health.

Our Beginnings

From Youth Outreach to
FQHC-Certified Health Center

1998

Humble Beginnings

Hope Family Care Center began as a youth outreach program in eastern Kansas City, driven by a vision for the holistic development of the community.

2006

Strategic Vision Expands

The HFCC Board embarked on a strategic planning process to expand the organization's impact — identifying accessible medical care as a pressing and unmet need in the area.

2013

Health Center Transformation

HFCC recognized the need to strengthen the structure and function of its health center and began pursuing Federally Qualified Community Health Center (FQHC) Look-Alike status.

2015

FQHC Look-Alike Status Achieved

In October 2015, HFCC successfully attained its FQHC Look-Alike designation — a landmark milestone that brought expanded access, resources, and hope to the underserved community.

Today

Serving Our East Side KC Community

HFCC continues to operate exclusively on grants, donations, and patient revenue — ensuring every member of our East Side Kansas City community has access to exceptional, preventative, personal health care, regardless of income.
